WA Web Sidebar Toggle is installed!

One last step — pin it so it's always a click away.

Pin to toolbar

Click the puzzle icon 🧩 in Chrome's toolbar → find WA Web Sidebar Toggle → click the pin icon. Done.

1

Open WhatsApp Web

Go to web.whatsapp.com — the toggle button appears in the left navigation rail automatically.

2

Click the toggle or press Alt + S

The sidebar collapses with a smooth animation. Works on every tab — Chats, Status, Communities, and Settings.

3

Press again to bring it back

The same button or shortcut toggles it back. Your state is remembered for the session.
